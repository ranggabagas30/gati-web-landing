## ADDED Requirements
### Requirement: About Us Section Styling
The About Us section (ManifestoSection) SHALL display with a primary orange background color (`#f05123`) to maintain brand consistency.

#### Scenario: About Us section displays with orange background
- **WHEN** the user views the landing page
- **THEN** the About Us section (identified by `id="about"`) displays with a background color of `#f05123` (primary orange)
- **AND** the text remains readable with appropriate contrast
